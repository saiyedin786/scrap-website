COMPANY PROFILE

AA Metal Scrap company was established in year 2007

AA Metal Scrap Company core business is trading of steel scrap metal both ferrous as well as non-ferrous and bridging the supply chain between steel mills and yards worldwide.

The Company was founded by Mr. Ankur Dubey.

We take pride to announce trading 2000 to 2500 tons of steel scrap per year including local and Overseas.

Our global presence and expertise enables us to cater to every market and customers worldwide.

We handle complete range of steel products including ferrous scrap, non-ferrous scrap, and ferroalloys.

We have our business worldwide including Singapore, Dubai, Hong Kong, Japan, South Korea, China, South Africa, Philippines, Thailand, Malaysia, Australia, USA, and several African, Carribean, Central and South American countries.

We readily welcome new markets and opportunities for procuring and supplying our products.
